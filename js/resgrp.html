Rishi Agarwal, M Tech Student (2015 - Current), BSBE

Rishi's work focuses on identification of new peroxisomal matrix proteins carrying non-canonical Peroxisomal targeting sequence in Saccharomyces cerevisiae (Yeast). Currently he is applying high-accuracy prediction methods to identify such proteins. In the next step, he would validate the predictions through wet lab experiments. (Peroxisome is a single layered subcellular organelle in eukaryotic cell encompassing a proteinaceous matrix. These matrix proteins are synthesized in nucleus and targeted to this organelle by their targeting signal sequence which is mainly PTS1 and PTS2.)
