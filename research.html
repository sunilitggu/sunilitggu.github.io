Current Project: Knowledge base Question Answering (KBQA)
Task Definition:
KBQA systems answer questions by obtaining information from knowledge bases (KB) such as, Freebase, DBpedia or Wikidata. For instance, the question "Who is the founder of Microsoft?" can be answered by issuing the KB query "founder_of(Microsoft, ?)". Automatically mapping a natural language question to its corresponding KB query is a challenging task.
