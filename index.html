About Me:
I am a Research Associate at National Centre of Text Mining (NaCTeM), The University of Manchester. I am advised by Prof. Sophia Ananiadou.
Prior to joining NaCTeM, I was a Ph.D. candidate in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering, IIT Guwahati, India, where I was fortunate to work with Dr. Ashish Anand in Natural Language Processing area.
