About Me:
Currently, I am a Research Associate at Inception Institute of Artificial Intelligence (IIAI). I am working on Knowledge Base Question Answering problem.
Prior to joining IIAI, I was a PostDoc at National Centre of Text Mining (NaCTeM), The University of Manchester with Prof. Sophia Ananiadou.
And before that, I was a Ph.D. candidate in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering, IIT Guwahati, India, working with Dr. Ashish Anand.
