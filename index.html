Curriculum Vitae
 I am a PhD candidate in Computer Science and Engineering Department at IIT Guwahati. I work as a student researcher in Machine Learning and Data Mining Group. I am advised by Dr. Ashish Anand.

My research interests include topics in Biomedical and Clinical Natural Language Processing. Currently I am working on Deep Learning models for information extraction in medical literatures.

Prior to joining IITG, I have completed my Master's in School of Computer and Information Science,University of Hyderabad, where I was fortunate to work with Prof. K Narayana Murthy in Natural Language Processing area.

And before that, I was a graduate student in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering, ITGGU Bilaspur .
